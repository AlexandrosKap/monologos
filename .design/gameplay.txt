First person room game
Move in a grid
Go to the exit and win
Searching for someone that was with you at the start

Click button to open the map
A map shows everything in the grid
Find your place in the map to avoid stuff
Map has dots that are enemies or you

Move counter
After N moves a chaser appears
They will kill all enemies first, then the player
The chaser ends the game

If an enemy is found, fight or run
Running will cost 4 moves and can fail
Fight is a game where you click right/left at a specific time
Good click = hit
Bas click = lose 2 hp
